Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the TrueMark process

Is my original file stored on the blockchain?

+

No, only a cryptographic hash (digital fingerprint) of your file is stored on the blockchain. Your original file remains private and secure.

How long does the process take?

+

The entire process typically takes less than 5 minutes. Hash generation is instant, and blockchain recording usually completes within 2-3 minutes.

Can certificates be forged or tampered with?

+

No, certificates cannot be forged or tampered with. They are cryptographically secured and verified against immutable blockchain records.

What happens if the blockchain network goes down?

+

We use multiple blockchain networks for redundancy. If one network experiences issues, your certificates remain verifiable on other networks.

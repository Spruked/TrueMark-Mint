Cookie Policy

Last Updated: October 2, 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help websites remember your preferences, improve your experience, and provide analytics about website usage.

2. How We Use Cookies

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly:

  • Session Management: Keep you logged in during your visit
  • Security: Prevent cross-site request forgery attacks
  • Preferences: Remember your language and accessibility settings

2.2 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website:

  • Google Analytics: Track page views, user behavior, and site performance
  • Performance Monitoring: Identify and fix technical issues
  • Usage Statistics: Understand popular features and content

2.3 Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience on our platform:

  • Wallet Connections: Remember your preferred crypto wallet
  • Network Preferences: Remember your blockchain network choice
  • UI Preferences: Remember your interface customizations

3. Managing Cookies

3.1 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block All Cookies: Disable all cookies (may impact functionality)
  • Block Third-Party Cookies: Allow only first-party cookies
  • Clear Cookies: Delete existing cookies from your device
  • Cookie Notifications: Get alerts before cookies are set

3.2 Platform Settings

In your TrueMark Mint account settings, you can:

  • Opt-out of analytics cookies
  • Disable functional cookies (with limited impact)
  • Clear platform-specific data

4. Third-Party Cookies

4.1 Service Providers

We use trusted third-party services that may set cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics and performance monitoring
  • Square: Payment processing (when using credit cards)
  • MetaMask: Wallet connection and transaction processing

4.2 Social Media

If you interact with social media features, those platforms may set cookies according to their own policies.

5. Cookie Retention

5.1 Session Cookies

Deleted when you close your browser or end your session.

5.2 Persistent Cookies

Remain on your device for a set period:

  • Preference Cookies: 1 year
  • Analytics Cookies: 2 years
  • Security Cookies: 30 days

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Significant changes will be communicated through:

  • Email notifications
  • Platform announcements
  • Website banners

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Getting Started

How do I create my first certificate?

+

Simply visit our Mint page, upload your digital asset, fill in the details, and follow the step-by-step process.

What file formats do you support?

+

We support all major file formats including images, documents, videos, audio files, and code files. There are no file size limits.

Technical Support

How can I verify a certificate?

+

Use the QR code or verification link on any certificate to access our verification portal and confirm authenticity.

Is my data secure?

+

Yes, we use bank-level encryption and only store cryptographic hashes on the blockchain, never your actual files.

Billing & Pricing

What are your pricing plans?

+

Basic certificates cost $7 each. We also offer volume discounts and enterprise plans for bulk authentication needs.

Do you offer refunds?

+

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with our service.
